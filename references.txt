Some General Instructions for using the App :- 
1. While creating new events, Event Type accepts only 4 entries :-
a) Study Plan
b) Exams
c) Lectures
d) Assignments
2. The date to be entered must be of the format dd.mm.yyyy
Eg. 08.12.2021 not 8.12.2021 or 08-12-2021
3. For deleting an event, just enter the name of the event to be deleted
